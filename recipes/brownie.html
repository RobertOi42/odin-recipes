Brownies

Recipe for simple chocolate brownies!

This recipe is for simple chocolate brownies!

Below are the ingredients necessary and the
steps for the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees F and grease a square pan
  2. Melt the butter and stir in sugar, eggs and vanilla extract.
